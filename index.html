I work where software engineering, data science, and deep learning meet: building Python-first AI systems that turn models into reliable tools. LLMs, RAG, NLP, computer vision, MLOps, and healthcare AI are the materials I keep shaping.

The Craft

For me, AI is not just an API to call; it is a material to understand, shape, and discipline. I enjoy harnessing model capability, giving agents a clear character and purpose, and turning raw intelligence into systems that can reason, act, and serve with restraint.

That craft sits somewhere between engineering and art: scientific enough to measure, practical enough to ship, and creative enough to give these new machine personalities a useful voice. The goal is never spectacle; it is elegant work that helps people.

Principles

  • Build with scientific curiosity and engineering discipline.
  • Support open source and the shared knowledge that moves us forward.
  • Use AI for human benefit, with respect for safety, privacy, and trust.
  • Prefer useful, trustworthy systems over impressive demos.
  • Keep learning, building, and improving the craft.
