I build practical AI systems with Python, language models, data, and disciplined engineering. My work lives around LLMs, RAG, NLP, computer vision, MLOps, and healthcare AI.

The Craft

I like code that turns ideas into working instruments. These days, that often means shaping model behavior, designing agent personalities, and giving LLMs the right tools, memory, and guardrails so they can move with purpose instead of noise.

There is a quiet art in this work: harnessing powerful models without worshipping them, making small agents useful, and letting systems become more reliable through careful constraints.

Principles

  • Support open source and the shared knowledge that moves us forward.
  • Use AI for human benefit, with respect for safety, privacy, and trust.
  • Prefer useful systems over impressive demos.
  • Keep learning, building, and improving the craft.
